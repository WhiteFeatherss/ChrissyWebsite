A bit of myself I originally joined College in Natural Sciences, although I have a strong affinity for science and the likes, I quickly realized that it wasn't the place for me. I still enjoy science and its wonders but from an science enthusiast point of view.

I then proceeded to join a program called Tremplin DEC, which I immediatly regretted. It is a program where you only basic course in College and nothing else, I've had no motivation to learn at that point.

After a year of sabatical leave to a program I hated, I thought I've had finally found my vocation and joined a program where I stayed for 2 years in, called 'Technologie de systèmes ordinés', the program consisted of repairing, maintaining and or replacing old/new electronical equipement, servers and much more. Programming was an integral part of the said course, I grew fond of it, I enjoyed the problem solving and spending hours to fix tiny mistakes that I would make along the way. Even though I loved it, it wasn't exactly what I was looking for. So I went once again on a year of sabatical leave and decided to get into Video Game Programming, where I am now.
